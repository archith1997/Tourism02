Hampton Inn Downtown

Located directly across Meridian Street from the Circle Centre mall, the Hampton Inn downtown is housed in the nine-story Chesapeake Building, a beautifully-restored historic structure originally constructed in 1929 to serve as a headquarters for the Big Four Railroad.
In additional to a complimentary hot breakfast buffet, all rooms offer high-speed Internet and free local calls (in addition to such tangible features as pillowtop mattresses, an iron and ironing board, and a hair dryer). Suites with two rooms are available. The hotel also offers meeting rooms with a 100-person capacity.

Omni Severin Hotel

40 West Jackson Place
Indianapolis, IN 46225

317-634-6664

Ideally located in the heart of downtown Indianapolis, the Omni Severin Hotel stands as the city’s longest running luxury hotel and a beautifully restored piece of the city’s history. The hotel elegantly combines the grandeur of the early 1900s with the modern amenities and superior service of today and offers memorable experiences that are within walking distance to many leisure, business, and cultural attractions. Just steps outside the front door, you can explore over 300 nearby shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues. The hotel is connected to Circle Centre mall and the Indiana Convention Center via skywalks and is just a short walk to Lucas Oil Stadium and Bankers Life Fieldhouse.

Enjoy a peaceful night in our Westin Indianapolis hotel, featuring the signature Westin Heavenly Bed®, and revive in the morning with our in-room coffee maker complete with complimentary Starbucks® coffee. Each of our spacious guest rooms and suites offer modern decor, fostering an environment conducive to rest and comfort. Let us take care of you with such services as concierge, gift shop, AGM and a 32" LGHD TV. Service Express connects you to whatever you need at the touch of a button. Whether you're seeking extra towels, in-room dining or recommendations for local activities and attractions, simply pick up the phone and we'll see to it that your request is fulfilled. Inject some energy into your day with a visit to our WestinWORKOUT® Fitness Center on the third floor. Tone and tighten with free weights and circuit machines; get your cardio on a treadmill, stationary bike or elliptical trainer. Afterwards, sink into the whirlpool to relieve those tired muscles and relax! The No Name Lounge is ideal for cocktails and light fare. Guests on the go can stop by Capitol Grounds in the hotel lobby for a freshly brewed latte and delicious pastries. Take advantage of complimentary wi-fi and comfortable leather chairs for a relaxing moment. Our downtown location puts you in the middle of the action, with countless restaurants, shops and clubs just outside the hotel doors.
